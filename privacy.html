Privacy Policy

Curtis Painting Integration — Last updated: April 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Curtis Painting Integration application ("App") handles information. This is a private, single-user internal business tool operated by Nik Curtis for Curtis Painting and Remodeling.

1. Data Collected

The App accesses the following data from QuickBooks Online solely to perform its business functions:

  • Customer names and contact information stored in QuickBooks Online
  • Item and service records stored in QuickBooks Online
  • Estimate and invoice data created through the App

2. How Data Is Used

All data accessed through the App is used exclusively to create and manage estimates and invoices for Curtis Painting and Remodeling. No data is sold, shared, or transmitted to any third party.

3. Data Storage

OAuth tokens required to connect to QuickBooks Online are stored locally on the operator's private machine and are not uploaded to any external server. No customer data is stored outside of QuickBooks Online.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the Intuit QuickBooks Online API. Data shared with QuickBooks Online is governed by Intuit's Privacy Statement.

5. No Public Access

This App is not available to the public. It is operated solely by the business owner and is not distributed to any end users.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated at any time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
